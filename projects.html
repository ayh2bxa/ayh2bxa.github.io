Filter-dependent point cloud audio visualisation

This audio visualisation work features a filter, whose shape can be drawn arbitrarily with your mouse, to control granularity of point cloud. The more low-frequency content the filter preserves, the larger an individual point. Background music used is an excerpt of my original work "20 Dreams", and the photo used for the visualisation is taken by myself.

Transformer voice effect

Inspired by this tutorial, I wrote a real-time C++ program to emulate the deep robotic voices of the Transformers. The samples used are chosen from FL Studio's built-in sample packs. The GUI is developed under the JUCE framework, the FFT is developed by Signalsmith, but all the remaining DSP algorithms and real-time optimisations are written by myself from scratch.
